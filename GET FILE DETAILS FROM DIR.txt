PowerShell (recommended)

Single folder:
Get-ChildItem -File | Select-Object FullName, Length

Recursive (all subfolders):
Get-ChildItem -File -Recurse | Select-Object FullName, Length

Human‑readable size and sorted by size:
Get-ChildItem -File -Recurse |
  Select-Object FullName,@{Name='Size';Expression={ "{0:N2} MB" -f ($_.Length/1MB)}} |
  Sort-Object @{Expression={[double]($_.Size -replace '[^0-9.]','')};Descending=$true}

Export to CSV:
Get-ChildItem -File -Recurse | Select-Object FullName,Length | Export-Csv -Path files.csv -NoTypeInformation
